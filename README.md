# Scientific Horizons of Innovative Technologies (SHIT)

## About the Journal

**Scientific Horizons of Innovative Technologies (SHIT)** is an open-access academic journal dedicated to the dissemination of innovative findings, unconventional explorations, instructive detours, and reflective insights encountered throughout the scientific research process.  

Rather than focusing solely on polished success, the journal aims to broaden the scientific horizon of researchers by presenting original ideas, unexpected observations, methodological reflections, and even well-documented missteps that may illuminate new directions or help others avoid hidden pitfalls. In addition, SHIT encourages the sharing of **research methodologies, intellectual experiences, and practical lessons** accumulated along the scientific journey, recognizing that the path of inquiry is often as valuable as its destination.  

We welcome submissions from all fields of science, engineering, and technology. Interdisciplinary perspectives, exploratory studies, negative results of genuine value, creative technical insights, and thoughtful discussions on scientific methodology are particularly encouraged.  

The journal is organized into two primary sections:

- **Scientific and Technological Discoveries** — reporting novel findings, experimental or theoretical advances, technical developments, and meaningful negative or unexpected results.  
- **Scientific Reflections** — sharing methodological insights, research philosophies, lessons learned, and critical thinking arising from real scientific practice.  

SHIT operates as a fully **open-access** publication.  
There are **no article processing charges**, and issues are released **irregularly**, following the natural rhythm of meaningful discovery rather than rigid publication schedules.  

Through openness, curiosity, and intellectual honesty, the journal seeks to foster a scientific culture in which both progress and productive failure contribute to collective advancement.

# 《创新科技的科学视野》国际期刊

## 关于本期刊

**Scientific Horizons of Innovative Technologies（SHIT）** 是一本开放获取的学术期刊，致力于传播在科学研究过程中产生的**创新性发现、非常规探索、具有启示意义的曲折经历，以及深刻的科学反思**。  

本期刊不仅关注最终的成功成果，更希望通过展示原创思想、意外观察、方法反思，乃至被充分记录的研究弯路，来**拓宽研究者的科学视野**，启发新的思路，或帮助他人**避开潜在的研究陷阱**。同时，我们也鼓励分享科研过程中的**方法论思考、实践经验与心得体会**，因为通往知识的道路本身同样具有重要价值。  

我们欢迎来自**各类理工科领域**的投稿，尤其鼓励：  

- 跨学科研究  
- 探索性工作  
- 具有真实学术价值的负结果  
- 富有创造性的技术见解  
- 关于科学方法与科研经验的深入思考  

本期刊设置两个主要栏目：  

- **Scientific and Technological Discoveries（科学技术发现）** —— 报道新的实验或理论成果、技术进展，以及具有意义的负结果或意外发现。  
- **Scientific Reflections（科学思考）** —— 分享研究方法论、科研理念、经验教训，以及源自真实科研实践的批判性思考。  

本期刊实行**完全开放获取（Open Access）**：  

- **不收取任何版面费**  
- **不定期出版**，遵循真正有意义的科学发现所呈现的自然节奏，而非固定周期。  

通过倡导开放、好奇与学术诚实，本期刊希望营造一种科学文化：  
**无论是进步，还是富有成效的失败，皆能共同推动人类知识的前沿。**
